Shipping

We offer the option to pack the GPU seperately from the PC during shipping.
For certain PCs with larger, 40 series graphics cards, we highly recommend choosing to have the GPU packed seperately during shipping.
Choosing to have the GPU either packed seperately or pre-installed does not affect the price or warranty of the PC.
A demonstration video pertaining to your specific build will be included with purchase and installation of GPU should take no more than 15 minutes.
A direct line of communication with a Super Admin is included with purchase, so you can feel free to call, text, or facetime the day your PC arrives with any questions.
All PCs are shipped same business day.

About + Warranty

Viirty is a custom prebuilt gaming PC company able to keep up with latest trends and styles without sacrificing build quality, offering much more flexible warranties and personal attention.
No contracts with major companies means absolutely no preinstalled "bloatware". No gamer hub applications, weird free antiviruses, unncessary browsers, or anything else.
Everything arrives ready to be customized by you.
All PCs come with a 3-year hardware and labor warranty. Warranty covers all original parts as well as software updates. In order to initiate a warranty request, please email hello@viirty.com. To learn more please click the link down below.

Extras

PC comes with inactive copy of Windows 11 Home. Activation key will be shipped on a physical sticker inside of the PC box, as well as the 4-digit login pin.
All spare hardware pertaining to the PC will be included as well as all the various manufacturer stickers that come with different components. Further details on specific items included will be listed on the "For Sale" page of each build.

For GPUs packed seperately
Insert GPU into top-most PCIe slot until "click" is heard
Slide in GPU support stand if applicable
Insert and tighten GPU side slot screws until snug
Plug power cable into GPU until completely flush (may require some wiggling)
For all PCs
Install glass side panel(s) for PC case
Plug power cable into back of PC and flip switch from 'O' to 'I' position
Turn PC on
Play Elden Ring

Plug the monitor cable into the GPU slot (the lowest slot available on the side of the PC), not the motherboard slot.
In general if faced with the option, DP > HDMI.
